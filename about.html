Who Am I?

I am a Web Developer whose primary focus is on front end and UI/UX technologies and WordPress development. The languages I am learning and using include: JavaScript, jQuery, AngularJS, and PHP. And of course HTML and CSS to pretty things up and Bootstrap to help with repsonsiveness.

Additionally, I offer copywriting, copy editing, and marketing services, including SEO and analytics.
